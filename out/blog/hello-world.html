Hello, World

Welcome to my blog. This is the first npost on the new platform ,,— powered by Keystatic CMS and rendered with Markdoc.

Why a blog?

I've been building software for a while now, and I wanted a place to share:

  • Deep dives into system design and architecture decisions!
  • Notes on event-driven architectures and distributed systems
  • Quick tips and tricks from the trenches

The stack

This blog is built with:

  1. Next.js — static export deployed to Cloudflare Pages
  2. Keystatic — a git-based CMS for managing content locally
  3. Markdoc — a Markdown-based authoring format from Stripe

All content lives as files in the repo, committed alongside the code. No database, no external CMS — just files and git.
